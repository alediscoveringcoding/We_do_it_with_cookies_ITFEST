PathFinder 
Find your perfect Romanian university based on who you are.

PathFinder helps high school students discover universities that match 
their personality, interests, and passions through a RIASEC-based 
assessment system.

 Features
University Match Quiz — 20 questions based on the RIASEC personality model
Smart Matching — universities ranked by compatibility score (0-4)
Discover Page — browse all 80+ Romanian universities with filters
Tinder-Style Swipe — swipe YES/NO through your matched universities
Skills Explorer — explore skills and find universities that teach them
Personal Dashboard — track your matches, shortlist, and career suggestions
Community Q&A — ask questions, get AI answers, reply to others
Persistent Sessions — all progress saved to your account

How the Matching Works
User answers 20 RIASEC questions
System calculates top 3 personality traits (e.g. I, A, S)
Universities are filtered — must share at least 2 traits
Results ranked by match score with dominant trait bonus
User swipes through matches to build final shortlist
